Mark F. Walker, M.D., is an Associate Professor of Neurology, at the Case Western Reserve University School of Medicine and a Staff Neurologist at the Louis Stokes Cleveland Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center. Dr. Walker is a Principal Investigator of the Daroff-Dell'Osso Ocular Motility Laboratory.
