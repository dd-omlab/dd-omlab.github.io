Alessandro Serra, MD, PhD

Dr. Serra's CV. (Zipped PDF)


Alessandro Serra is a Case Western Reserve University Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Daroff-Dell'Osso Ocular Motility Laboratory. His research interests are saccade pathophysiology, eye-movement disorders, in particular saccade abnormalities, congenital and acquired nystagmus.
