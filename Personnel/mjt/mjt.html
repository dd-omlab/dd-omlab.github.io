Matthew J. Thurtell, MBBS

Curriculum Vitae & Bibliography (PDF) (HTML)      

Matthew J. Thurtell, MBBS, is a Neuro-Ophthalmology Fellow at the University Hospitals Case Medical Center. Dr. Thurtell is a Fellow of the Daroff-Dell'Osso Ocular Motility Laboratory. His research interests are saccade kinematics, vestibulo-ocular reflex kinematics, eye-movement disorders, and clinical neuro-ophthalmology.
